Green Bean Fries

Lightly battered green bean fries.

Here is a recipe to turn ordinary green beans into delicious crunchy fries.

All we need is the help of our trusty air fryer.

Ingredients:

  • Green Beans
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Cooking Spray
  • Lemon Wedges
